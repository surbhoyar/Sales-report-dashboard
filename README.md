# Sales-report-dashboard
The dashboard provides a consolidated view of key sales KPIs to support data-driven decision-making for business stakeholders.
The report highlights total sales, total quantity sold, profit, and growth trends using visually intuitive charts and KPIs. Users can track monthly and yearly sales performance, identify top-performing products and regions, and analyze customer contribution to overall revenue.

Interactive slicers and filters allow users to drill down by date, region, category, and product, enabling detailed analysis and quick insights. Advanced DAX measures are used to calculate metrics such as Year-to-Date (YTD) sales, Month-over-Month (MoM) growth, and profit margin.

This dashboard helps sales managers and executives:

Monitor overall business performance

Identify sales trends and seasonal patterns

Detect underperforming regions or products

Support strategic planning and forecasting

The solution is optimized for performance and follows Power BI best practices in data modeling, visualization design, and user experience.
